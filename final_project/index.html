Find your new best friend!

Paws Animal Rescue connects loving families with pets in need of a forever home.

Why Adopt with Us?

We are a non-profit rescue dedicated to matching pets with caring homes. Every adoption includes:

  • Vaccinations and a basic health check
  • Spay/Neuter surgery and microchipping
  • Post-adoption support if you have any questions

How Adoption Works

  1. Browse our adoptable pets.
  2. Submit a quick online application.
  3. Meet your new friend at the shelter.
  4. Complete paperwork and bring them home.
