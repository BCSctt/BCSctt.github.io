Get Involved

Paws Animal Rescue depends on volunteers, foster homes, and donations to care for pets until they find their forever families. There are many ways you can help, big or small.

Ways You Can Help

Volunteer at the Shelter

Spend a few hours a week walking dogs, playing with cats, cleaning kennels, or helping at the front desk.

  • Dog walking and basic training
  • Cat socializing and grooming
  • Cleaning and laundry
Ask about volunteering

Become a Foster

Open your home temporarily to a pet that needs extra care, space to recover, or a break from the busy shelter.

  • Short-term and long-term options
  • We provide supplies when we can
  • Great for pets needing quiet homes
Ask about fostering

Donate Supplies or Funds

Donations help us cover food, vet visits, and everyday items that keep the animals safe and comfortable.

  • Monthly giving or one-time gifts
  • Food, toys, and cleaning supplies
  • Corporate or school donation drives
Ask about donating

Shelter Wish List

If you prefer to donate items, here are some of the things we use every day:

  • Canned and dry dog and cat food
  • Leashes, collars, and harnesses
  • Clean blankets and towels
  • Cat litter and litter boxes
  • Dog and cat toys (washable if possible)
  • Cleaning supplies (unscented bleach, paper towels)
