About Paws Animal Rescue

Paws Animal Rescue is a fictional, volunteer-powered shelter created for a website design project. The goal of this site is to show how a real rescue might share its mission, animals, and adoption process online.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: connect loving families with pets that need a second chance. We imagine a world where every dog and cat has a safe, caring home and no animal is left behind in a shelter.

  • Rescue dogs and cats from unsafe or overcrowded situations.
  • Provide medical care, vaccinations, and microchipping.
  • Support adopters with guidance before and after adoption.

Our Story

Paws Animal Rescue was "founded" in 2025 as part of an introductory programming class. While the organization is not real, the types of information and pages you see on this site are similar to what many real rescues use every day.

Building this website is a way to practice HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap While imagining how technology can help animals find homes faster.
