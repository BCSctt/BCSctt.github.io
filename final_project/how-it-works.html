How Adoption Works

We want the adoption process to be friendly and clear for both you and the animals. Here is a simple overview of each step from your first visit to bringing your new friend home.

Step-by-Step Process

1. Browse Pets

Start by looking through our Adoptable Pets page and make a list of any animals you'd like to meet.

2. Submit an Application

Fill out a short adoption form so we can learn about your home, experience with pets, and what you're looking for.

3. Meet & Greet

Visit the shelter to spend time with the pet. Our staff will answer questions and help you decide if it's a good match.

4. Finalize & Take Home

After approval, you'll complete the paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and take your new best friend home.

What You Need To Adopt

To keep our animals safe and make sure you're ready, we ask adopters to:

  • Be at least 18 years old (or accompanied by a legal guardian).
  • Provide a valid photo ID with current address.
  • Have landlord approval if you rent your home.
  • Introduce all family members to the pet when possible.
  • Agree to basic care, training, and veterinary needs.

What to Expect on Adoption Day

Adoption day is exciting! Here's a sample timeline of what usually happens:

Time Activity
0 - 15 minutes Check in, review your application, and confirm the pet you're adopting.
15 - 30 minutes Spend a little more time with the pet and ask any final questions.
30 - 45 minutes Complete paperwork and go over medical/vaccination records.
45 - 60 minutes Pay the adoption fee, take a "Gotcha Day" photo, and head home together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I adopt if I already have pets?

Yes. We may ask for proof of current vaccinations and recommend a slow introduction between your current pets and your new one.

What is included in the adoption fee?

All pets are up to date on core vaccinations, spayed or neutered when possible, and microchipped. Some may also include starter food or supplies.
