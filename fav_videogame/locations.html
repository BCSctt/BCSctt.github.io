Levels & Locations

Elden Ring doesn't use “levels” in the traditional sense of linear stages; instead, it unfolds across a vast open world called the Lands Between, which is divided into distinct regions. Each location has its own geography, history, and visual identity, while also housing legacy dungeons, large, traditional FromSoftware-style levels filled with traps, bosses, and lore.

Beyond these, there are countless smaller dungeons, catacombs, caves, and ruins scattered across the map, making exploration essential. Each region reflects Elden Ring's core themes of decay, ambition, and the collapse of once-great orders while offering distinct challenges and atmospheres.
